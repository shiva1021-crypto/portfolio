Authors: Shivaprasath, Co-author S. Yogesh Reddy, Manoj A, Monika, Deepashree

International Conference on IoT Based Control Networks and Intelligent Systems (ICICNIS)

The Biometric Voting System modernizes state elections by allowing voters to cast votes from any nearby booth, regardless of their registered constituency. It ensures secure verification using facial recognition, fingerprint authentication (R307), RFID, and a microcontroller like Raspberry Pi.
